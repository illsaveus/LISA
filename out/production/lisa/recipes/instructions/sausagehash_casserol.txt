1.Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease baking dish with butter.
2.Beat eggs, sour cream, milk, and salt in a bowl until blended. Stir in onions. Pour mixture in the prepared baking dish.
3.Bake in the preheated oven until set, 25 to 30 minutes. Sprinkle cheese over eggs and continue baking until cheese is melted, 2 to 3 minutes more.
